Welcome to Government Polytechnic, Srinagar

Established in 1968, Government Polytechnic Srinagar Garhwal is affiliated with the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education. The institute is committed to academic excellence, offering top-notch diploma engineering programs.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for excellence in Diploma Engineering & Diploma Pharmacy Education, producing globally competent professionals.

Mission

  • To provide state - of- art infrastructural facilities that support achieving Academic Excellence..
  • To provide a work environment that is conducive for professional growth of faculty and staff.
  • To collaborate with industry for achieving excellence in Training and Placement.
